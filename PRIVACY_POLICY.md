**W-Link ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy.**

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the W-Link application. Simply put: **We do not collect or store any of your personal data.**

### 1. Data Collection and Use

W-Link is a **local network controller**.

* **Local Communication**: The App communicates directly with your WLED devices (e.g., ESP8266/ESP32 controllers) solely within your local network (Wi-Fi).
* **No Cloud Upload**: All control commands, settings, and device lists are stored locally on your device or sent directly to the WLED hardware. We do not operate a cloud server and do not upload any of your data to the internet.
* **No Tracking**: We do not use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Flurry, etc.) to track your behavior or collect device identifiers.

### 2. Network Permissions

The App requires access to your Local Network permissions strictly for:

* Discovering WLED devices on your LAN via the mDNS protocol.
* Sending HTTP requests to devices to control lighting.

### 3. Data Security

Since all data remains on your personal device and local network, the security of your privacy primarily depends on the security of your own network environment.

### 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues:
<https://github.com/fkueyu/W-Link>

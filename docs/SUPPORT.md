### FAQ

**Q: Why can't I find my device?**
A: Please check the following:

1. Your phone and WLED device are connected to the **same Wi-Fi network**.
2. You have granted the **"Local Network"** permission to the app (iOS Settings -> W-Link).
3. Your WLED device is powered on and running.

**Q: Does it support remote control (outside home)?**
A: No. For security and privacy reasons, W-Link is designed to work strictly within your local network.
